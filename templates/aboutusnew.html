About Summer Intenship 2022

For second-year students, the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Poornima Institute of Engineering and Technology (PIET) offered a 45-day Summer Internship in Data Science and Machine Learning. This environment allowed us to develop our ideas and conduct research and development. The National Board of Accreditation (NBA) granted PIET accreditation for the academic years 2017–2018. Because of their collaborative efforts and creative ideas, PIET has achieved amazing milestones.
