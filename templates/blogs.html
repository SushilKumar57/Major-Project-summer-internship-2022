memorable experiences during company visit

On 3rd August, 2022 went on an industrial visit to Bhamashah Techno Hub, Jaipur. It was amazing experience! 3D games!! Great use of technology! 3D movie theatre!! Amazing infrastructure!! 3D Photo-shoot. Its an amazing place where you can see a lot of knowledge and new technologies. Here is a 7d cinema which introduces a new incredible technique. Here is vr technology that can help peoples to engage with newly high technology. VR Games are awesome. Really enjoyed a lot.

On 5th August, 2022 went on an industrial visit to Auriga IT consulting private limited compamay, Jaipur Very good environment for work. Nice people & employee centric organization. Office premises is surrounded with nature which make it different from concrete towers. Love this experience.
